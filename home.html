Diploma in Engineering

A Diploma in Engineering is a practical and hands-on course that typically lasts three years and is divided into six semesters. This program is designed to equip students with the technical skills and knowledge needed for various engineering fields. It is an excellent option for those who want to enter the workforce quickly or who plan to continue their education with a bachelor's degree in engineering.
